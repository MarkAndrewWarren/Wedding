How we first met / Unoffical Date

"We first met through our mutual friends Sean and Sarah. Getting together for a game night, I was irresistible to her with my drunken angry ramblings. We truly connected on our first unofficial date at the renaissance festival, were we were so engaged in our own conversation we kept wandering away and losing our group (Sean and Sarah again). We spent the whole day talking and couldn't wait to speak again" - Mark

"Ok, I remember us first meeting at a game night at Sean and Sarah's house. We were playing a game involving lying and deception called Secret Hitler. I had never played the game before, but I had played similar ones, so I understood the concept. I was one of the fascists (the bad guys the other players are trying to uncover), but I played innocent well, claiming any misstep I had was because I was new to the game. Only Mark was onto me, and I greatly enjoyed beating him. After that, he invited me to his company cabin along with other friends for a weekend full of board games, where I enjoyed playing against him again (and winning). He didn't quite pick up the hint that I liked him at that point, but when we went to the Renaissance Festival with Sean and Sarah, and spent the whole day talking, I think he figured it out" - Michelle
